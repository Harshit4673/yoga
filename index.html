Famous Yoga Poses

Sukhasana

Sit cross-legged on a yoga mat with your hands on your knees, palms up. Keep your spine as straight as you can.This pose also boosts back flexibility and can help relieve stress.

Vrksasana

Bring your hands together in the prayer position and lift them over your head. Balance on your right leg. Bend your left knee out to the left side and press your left foot to the inner thigh of your right leg or just your ankle

Adho Mukha Svanasana

Starting on hands and knees, then lifting the hips upward to create an V-shape with the body. This pose stretches and strengthens multiple muscle groups,like the shoulders, hamstrings, calves, and arches of the feet.

Janu Sirsasana

While performing this pose, the trunk is bent forward so the head touches the knee in the final posture, and hence the name Janu-sirs-asana. It involves forward bend, twist, and side stretch of the body.

Trikonasana

Trikonasana is a standing posture of the Hatha Yoga type of Yoga. 'Tri' means three and 'Kona' means 'corner'. So, trikonasana literally translates to 'Three-corners'. Final posture resembles a triangle

Bhujangasana

Bhujangasana or Cobra Pose is a reclining back-bending asana in hatha yoga and modern yoga as exercise. It is commonly performed in a cycle of asanas in Surya Namaskar, Salute to the Sun
